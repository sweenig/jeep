ABOUT ME

Jeep Wave

Hey there. Glad you made it here. You may have come here because my driver left a decal on your rig. We liked your Jeep, so we decided to send out some love. You can use the decal on your Jeep's window, body, your other ride, or even the back of your cell phone. If you need instructions on how to apply your decal, have no fear: the interwebs is here. First, go get some details about how decals are different than stickers. If you still need some help getting your decal applied, don't worry, there are more interwebs out there.
